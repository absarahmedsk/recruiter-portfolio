# Boolean & X-Ray Search Strings (Examples)

These are sample search strings I use for sourcing across US, India, and UAE markets.

---

## Frontend (US)
site:linkedin.com/in ("front end developer" OR "frontend developer" OR "frontend engineer") AND ("React" OR "Vue" OR "Angular") AND ("JavaScript" OR "TypeScript") AND ("San Francisco" OR "New York" OR "Seattle") -jobs

---

## Backend (India)
site:linkedin.com/in ("backend developer" OR "backend engineer") AND ("Java" OR "Spring" OR "Node.js") AND ("Bengaluru" OR "Bangalore" OR "Hyderabad" OR "Pune") -jobs

---

## QA / Test (UAE)
site:linkedin.com/in ("QA engineer" OR "QA tester" OR "automation tester") AND ("Selenium" OR "Cypress" OR "TestNG") AND ("Dubai" OR "Abu Dhabi") -jobs
