# Sourcing Tools and Chrome Extensions I Use

These are tools and browser extensions that help with sourcing automation, contact discovery, and productivity.

---

## Research & Sourcing Platforms
- **AmazingHiring** – Deep research tool for tech profiles
- **HireEZ (formerly Hiretual)** – Enrichment and AI-powered sourcing
- **SeekOut** – Diversity sourcing and GitHub/StackOverflow integration
- **OctoHR / Gitlead** – Developer-centric sourcing (GitHub-based)

---

## Email Finding & Verification
- **Hunter.io** – Find and verify candidate emails
- **ContactOut** – Chrome extension for LinkedIn email discovery
- **RocketReach** – Enrichment for cross-platform data
- **Snov.io** – Verify emails and track outreach

---

## Automation & Productivity
- **Phantombuster** – Automate small sourcing and LinkedIn tasks
- **Instant Data Scraper** – Quick, no-code data collection
- **TexAu** – Similar to Phantombuster for lightweight automations
- **Session Buddy** – Manage multiple sourcing tabs
- **Link Grabber** – Extract links from search pages
- **JSON Formatter** – Read JSON when exploring APIs or data files
