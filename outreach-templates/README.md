# Outreach Templates for Recruiters

This section includes sample LinkedIn and email templates used for engaging candidates in tech and non-tech roles across multiple markets (US, India, UAE, and EU).

---

## 1. LinkedIn Outreach (Tech Candidate – Software Engineer)

Hi [First Name],

I came across your profile and was impressed by your background in [specific skill or technology].  
We’re currently hiring for a [Job Title] role at [Company Name], where your experience with [Skill] could make a great impact.

Would you be open to a quick chat this week to explore if it aligns with your career goals?

Warm regards,  
Absar Ahmed Shaikh.

---

## 2. Email Outreach (Passive Candidate – Data Role)

Subject: Exploring a Data opportunity that matches your skillset

Hi [First Name],

I hope you’re doing well. I’m reaching out regarding an exciting [Job Title] position at [Company Name].  
Your experience with [Tool/Technology] caught my attention, and I believe this role could be a strong fit.

Would you be open to a quick 10-minute conversation to discuss further?

Warm regards,  
Absar Ahmed Shaikh.

---

## 3. Follow-up Message (When No Response)

Hi [First Name],

Just wanted to follow up in case my previous message got buried in your inbox.  
I’d still love to connect and share more details about the [Job Title] role.

Looking forward to hearing from you.  

Warm regards,  
Absar Ahmed Shaikh.

---

## 4. Thank You Note (After Interview)

Hi [First Name],

Thank you for taking the time to speak with me today about the [Job Title] position.  
It was great to learn more about your experience and interests.

I’ll update you as soon as I have feedback from the hiring team.

Warm regards,  
Absar Ahmed Shaikh.
